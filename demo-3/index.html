Send Emails Directly From Your Code

No server code needed. Focus on things that matter!

How does it work?

Connect your email service

Choose from a wide variety of email services. We support both transactional email services (Mailgun, Mailjet, Mailtrap, SendinBlue, SendGrid, Amazon SES and Postmark) and personal email services (AOL, Gmail, FastMail, iCloud, Mail.ru, Outlook, Yahoo, Yandex and Zoho).

Create email template

Easily build your own template. Choose the tool most suitable for you: text editor or code editor. Templates are parameterized so that you can further customize them from your code.

Send email from your code

Add one of our SDK, and start sending emails! var params = { name: 'John', reply_email: 'john@example.com', message: 'This is awesome!' }; emailjs.send( 'gmail', 'feedback', params );

Summary

EmailJS is a service that allows you to send emails directly from your code. It is a great tool for developers who want to focus on things that matter. With EmailJS, you can connect your email service, create email templates, and send emails from your code. EmailJS supports a wide variety of email services, including transactional email services and personal email services. EmailJS is easy to use and provides a simple API that allows you to send emails with just a few lines of code. Try EmailJS today and see how easy it is to send emails directly from your code!
